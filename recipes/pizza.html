Odin's Pizza

Odin's Pizza is the best pizza in the world. It's made with the finest ingredients and is baked to perfection. It's the best pizza you'll ever have.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Roll out dough.
  3. Spread sauce on dough.
  4. Add cheese.
  5. Add toppings.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes.
