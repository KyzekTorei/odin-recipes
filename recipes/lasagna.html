Odin's Lasagna

Odin's Lasagna is the best lasagna in the world. It's made with the finest ingredients and is baked to perfection. It's the best lasagna you'll ever have.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Spread small amount of sauce in bottom of baking dish.
  3. Layer Noodles and sauce
  4. Top with cheese.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes.
